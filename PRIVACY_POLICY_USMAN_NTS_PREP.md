# Privacy Policy — UsmanNTSPrep

**Effective date:** 2026-02-27  
**App name:** UsmanNTSPrep (Offline-first NTS NAT preparation)

This Privacy Policy explains how UsmanNTSPrep (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the app.

---

## 1) Key points
- The app is designed to work **offline-first**.
- Your study activity (attempts, scores, progress) is stored **locally on your device**.
- No account/login is required.
- We do **not** sell personal information.
- If/when ads are enabled (e.g., Google AdMob), Google may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and ad interaction data under Google’s policies.

---

## 2) Information we collect

### A) Information you provide
We do **not** ask you to provide personal information (such as name, email, phone number) to use the app.

### B) Information stored on your device (offline data)
To provide practice/exam, review, dashboard insights, and PDFs, the app stores data **on your device**, such as:
- attempts and scores
- question outcomes (correct/incorrect/unattempted)
- progress and analytics used by review and dashboard screens
- generated PDF files (saved on-device)

### C) Automatically collected information (only for online features)
Some actions use the internet, such as:
- opening the Play Store for rating
- sharing the app link

If ads are enabled, ad providers may collect:
- device information (model, OS version)
- identifiers like **Advertising ID** (where available/allowed)
- IP address and approximate location (depending on provider configuration)
- ad interaction data (impressions/clicks)

---

## 3) Ads and third-party services

### Google AdMob (only if/when enabled)
If we enable ads, we may use **Google AdMob**. AdMob/Google may collect and process information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Google policies:
- https://policies.google.com/privacy
- https://developers.google.com/admob

### Google Play / Android services
When installed from Google Play or when using Play-related features, Google may process certain data under its own policies.

---

## 4) How we use information
We use locally stored study data to:
- generate practice/exam sessions
- show results and review insights
- compute dashboard analytics
- generate PDFs for your review

If ads are enabled, ad-related data is used by the ad provider to:
- deliver ads
- measure performance
- prevent fraud/abuse

---

## 5) Sharing of information
- We do **not** sell or rent personal information.
- We do **not** share your local study data with third parties as a business practice.
- If ads are enabled, the ad SDK may share information as described in its policies.

### Sharing you choose
If you use Android’s Share feature, the recipient/service you select will handle that shared data under their own privacy policy.

---

## 6) Data retention and deletion
- Study/progress data is stored **on your device**.
- Uninstalling the app typically removes local data.
- You can also clear the app’s storage in Android Settings to delete local data.

---

## 7) Security
We take reasonable steps to keep the app reliable and avoid collecting unnecessary data. No system is 100% secure.

---

## 8) Children’s privacy
UsmanNTSPrep is an education app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 9) Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, when ads or analytics are added). Updates will be posted in this repository and/or the app listing.
