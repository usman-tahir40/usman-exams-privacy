# Privacy Policy — Usman Exams

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Usman Exams is an offline-first MCQ practice app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

## Information we collect
### 1) Data you provide in the app
- **Profile information** (for example, a profile name you create in the app).

### 2) Data created by using the app
- **Practice and exam results** (attempt history, scores, analytics) stored on your device.
- **App settings** (selected board/class, preferences, Pro status stored locally).

### 3) Purchases (Play Store build only)
If you use the Play Store version, Google Play Billing handles payments. The app receives purchase/entitlement status from Google Play to unlock Pro features.

## How we use information
We use data only to:
- Provide app features (practice, exams, results, insights, PDFs).
- Save your progress and settings on your device.
- Enable and restore Pro access when applicable.

## Data storage
- The app is designed to work **offline**.
- Your profiles, settings, and exam history are stored **locally on your device**.
- We do not sell your personal information.

## Data sharing
We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:
- **Google Play Billing** (Play Store build) for purchase processing and entitlement verification.

## Children’s privacy
The app is an education app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to use the app offline (such as a local profile name).

## Security
We take reasonable steps to protect data stored in the app. However, no method of storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices
You can:
- Delete app data by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.
- Remove profiles and local history using in-app controls (where available).
